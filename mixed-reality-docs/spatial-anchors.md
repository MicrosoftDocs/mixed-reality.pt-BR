## <a name="best-practices"></a><span data-ttu-id="7e59d-121">Práticas recomendadas</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e59d-121">Best practices</span></span>

### <a name="always-render-anchored-holograms-within-3-meters-of-their-anchor"></a><span data-ttu-id="7e59d-129">Sempre processe hologramas ancorados a 3 metros de sua âncora</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e59d-129">Always render anchored holograms within 3 meters of their anchor</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7e59d-130">Âncoras espaciais estabilizam o sistema de coordenadas perto da origem da âncora.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e59d-130">Spatial anchors stabilize their coordinate system near the anchor's origin.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e59d-131">Se você processar hologramas a mais de cerca de 3 metros da origem, esses hologramas podem enfrentar erros perceptíveis de posicionamento de forma proporcional à distância da origem, devido a efeitos da alavanca arm.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e59d-131">If you render holograms more than about 3 meters from that origin, those holograms may experience noticeable positional errors in proportion to their distance from that origin, due to lever-arm effects.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e59d-132">Isso funciona se o usuário fica perto da âncora, pois o holograma também está longe do usuário, o que significa que o erro angular do holograma distante será pequeno.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e59d-132">That works if the user stands near the anchor, since the hologram is far away from the user too, meaning the angular error of the distant hologram will be small.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e59d-133">No entanto, se o usuário anda até o holograma distante, ele ficará grande na visão dele, tornando óbvios os efeitos da alavanca arm da origem da âncora distante.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e59d-133">However, if the user walks up to that distant hologram, it will then be large in their view, making the lever-arm effects from the faraway anchor origin quite obvious.</span></span>
